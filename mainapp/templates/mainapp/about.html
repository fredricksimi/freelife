About Us


Who we are

FREELIFE is a non-profit organization focusing on providing help on suicide prevention, substance abuse & addiction, gender based violence and among others by offering emotional support to those who may be in distress and therefore in danger of being is any of the said situations as well as creating awareness within communities. It is so far, one of the few organizations i with a specific focus on suicide prevention, depression & prevention and substance use & addiction.




Our Vision

A society in which fewer people thrive with no depression, drug addiction, gender based vioelnce and attempted suicide.



Our Mission

To provide confidential emotional support for people who are experiencing feelings of distress or despair and through focused programs create awareness among people
